Efficiency

Real time adjustments to maintain patient position

Make the most out of your movement tracking data, add the component that corrects it instantaneously.
CDR System knows how valuable time is for patients and therapists. With an automated and immediate patient repositioning technology, treatments can, now, be uninterrupted, faster and more efficient. With RTMM, the timeliness of treatment processes stops depending on patient immobilization.

Comfort

Advanced technology devoted to patient comfort

Patients know how critical treatment accuracy and precision are. The undeniable reliance on their own immobility is therefore source of great stress and anxiety. Providing them with a system that manages and corrects their movements proves to be tremendously reassuring.
In addition to reducing stress, RTMM helps patients feel comfortable throughout the treatment. With a noninvasive solution and a much freer patient, sensations of restraint turn into feelings of care.

Designed to be easily installed, RTMM is light weight, compact and compatible with your current benches set up and dedicated spaces. Thanks to an Ease-of-Use focused development, the day-to-day usage of the solution is intuitive and connects to your OSC systems. Its compatibility with your current devices and workflows makes it the solution that fits your current needs.
